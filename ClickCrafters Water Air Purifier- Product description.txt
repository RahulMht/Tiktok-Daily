Key Features:

The Vortex Wash: Watch the water swirl in real-time as it traps dust, pet dander, and impurities. The transparent water basin allows you to see the filtration process in action, providing satisfying visual proof that your air is getting cleaner.

Customizable Ambiance: Features a vibrant 7-color LED light system. Cycle through the colors for a dynamic look, or lock it on your favorite shade to match your room's aesthetic, nightstand, or desk setup.

The Setup Routine:

Fill the basin with clean tap water up to the indicated line.

Use a dropper to add your preferred essential oil or fragrance to the water.

Power on the device and select your preferred LED color.

Let the water vortex pull in stale air and release fresh, scented air back into your space.
