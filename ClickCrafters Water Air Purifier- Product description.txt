# Product Brief: ClickCrafters Vortex Water Air Purifier

## The Visual Hero: The Vortex Wash
The core visual anchor is the clear water basin holding tap water. When powered on, a miniature water tornado spins inside the transparent vessel. Floating particles, dust, and pet dander are pulled into the spinning liquid and trapped. There are no paper filters; dirty water is poured directly down a sink drain to reveal a clean basin.

## Sensory & Ambient Lighting
A vertical glow system illuminates the water basin. It offers seven solid glowing colors (Blue, Green, Purple, Red, Cyan, Yellow, White) and a dynamic cycling mode. It runs with a gentle, satisfying liquid bubble sound (natural ASMR water friction).

## Physical Interactions & Scent-Scaping
Users unscrew the lid or lift off the top dome of the unit. Drops of amber-colored essential oil or solid laundry scent beads are poured directly down into the tap water basin. When turned on, the water-based diffusion releases a fine, scent-rich vapor without pushing a heavy wet mist into the air.

## Target Demographics & Core Desires
- Pet Owners: Want to eliminate dog/cat smells, dander, and litter box odors.
- Light Sleepers: Want soothing white noise acoustics combined with humidified air for sleeping.
- Allergy Sufferers: Want to clear sinuses, eliminate morning stuffiness, and capture floating dust.
- Home Estheticians: Love room resets, organizing, cozy nightstand setups, and sensory aromatherapy.
