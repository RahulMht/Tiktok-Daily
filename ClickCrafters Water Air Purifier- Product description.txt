## What it is
A water-based air revitalizer and aroma diffuser. You fill the clear basin with ordinary tap water; when it runs, the water moves in a gentle swirl that draws airborne dust, pet dander, and odor particles down into the liquid, where they're trapped. There are no paper filters — when the water gets dirty you simply pour it down the sink and refill. A built-in 7-color LED softly lights the basin.

## The visual hero — the gentle water swirl (KEEP IT SOFT)
The anchor shot is the clear basin with the water swirling. **This is a small, soft, soothing swirl with light surface bubbling — not a dramatic tornado, not a violent spin, not a whirlpool.** Render it calm, slow, and mesmerizing. Particles drift toward the moving water and disappear under the surface. Any prompt that describes a "tight vortex," "tornado," "explosive swirl," or hard spinning is wrong for this product.

## Design & variants (match the connected reference image used)
The unit is a matte-black, textured, dome-shaped top with curved air slits, seated on a transparent bulbous water basin. The ClickCrafters logo appears on both the dome and the basin. Render the variant shown in whichever reference image is bound:

- **Monochrome:** matte-black dome on a fully clear, transparent basin. Minimalist, clean.
- **7-Color LED glow:** the LED lights the water from within — most commonly a soft violet/blue glow that casts a gentle colored light onto the basin and the surrounding surface.
- **White-core:** transparent base with a clean white cylindrical core visible inside the water chamber.

## Sound
A gentle, satisfying liquid bubbling — natural ASMR water friction. Quiet enough to run while sleeping.

## Aromatherapy interaction
The dome **lifts off** (it is not screwed on tight). The user lifts the dome, adds a few drops of amber essential oil or a few solid scent beads directly into the tap water, then sets the dome back on. Running, it releases a light, scent-rich vapor — no heavy wet mist.

- **Light sleepers** — soft white-noise bubbling plus fresher air for sleep.
- **Allergy sufferers** — clearer sinuses, less morning stuffiness, less floating dust.
- **Home estheticians** — cozy nightstand resets, organizing, sensory aromatherapy and self-care.
